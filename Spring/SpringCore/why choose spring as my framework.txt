why choose spring as my framework? 
1. mature, battle tested: 
spring has been around since 2003, huge bank's, e-commerce platforms, governments, and SaaS companies run it in production.
this means -> problems you hit, someone already solved it, enormous documentation, stable upgrade paths, predictable behavior under load

2.Ecosystem is gigantic: 
it connects you to ready-made modules for: 
database(JDBC, JPA),
security & OAuth
messaging(Kafka, RabbitMQ)
batch processing
clous & microservices
so instead of building infrastructure, you assemble solutions

3. Spring IoC container helps manage object creation and wiring
you'll get loose coupling, easier testing, cleaner architecture, replaceable components

DOWNSIDE: 
complex due to vast features, potential for performance overhead
