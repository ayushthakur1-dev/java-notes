while processing a high volume of data, we process it in batch instead of processing everything at once

everything revolves around a few building blocks

Job -> a container for the entire batch process

Step -> a phase of jab (eg. read->process->write)

Chunk processing: handle items in small group instead of one giant transaction 
eg. read100->process->write->commit->next100

ItemReader/ ItemProcess/ ItemWriter

Job Repository: 
- what ran
- when
- success/failures
- restart position
