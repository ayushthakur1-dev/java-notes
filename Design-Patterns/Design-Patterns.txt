What? -> typical solution to common problems in software design 
when a solution gets repeated over and over in various projects, someone eventually puts a name to them

Benefits? -> tries and tested solutions

Keep in mind -> having to know about pattern doesn't mean you have to apply them everywhere, sometime simple code is better

Types of patterns: 
1. Creational: provides object creation mechanism the increases flexibility and reuse of existing code
2. Structural: explains how to assemble objects and classes into larger structures, keeping the structure flexible 
3. Behavioral: take care of effective communication and the assignments of responsibilities between objects

Singleton
