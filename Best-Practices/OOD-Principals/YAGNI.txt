You Ain't Gonna Need It: refrain from implementing functionality until it is deemed necessary. 

Avoid premature optimization, or over-engineering in software development, focus on delivering only the features that are required to meet current business needs, rather than anticipating future requirements that may never materialize.

Advantages: 
Reduced Complexity
Faster time to market
Flexibility and Adaptability: make decisions based on actual user feedback and changing business priorities

Disadvantages: 
Lack of future proofing
Incomplete or inefficient solution
